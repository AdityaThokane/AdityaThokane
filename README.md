### 👋 About Me

I'm a **certified OT SOC & Threat Hunting Engineer** with **7+ years** of experience defending critical infrastructure and enterprise environments across OT, SIEM, EDR, and Email Security stacks.

- 🛡️ Currently a **Senior Engineer** at **1898 & Co. (Burns & McDonnell)**, leading threat hunting and OT-SOC operations for industrial clients
- 🏭 Deep hands-on expertise across **ICS/SCADA**, the **Purdue Model**, and tools like **Claroty**, **Dragos**, and **Armis**
- 🔭 Skilled at mapping real-world attacks to **MITRE ATT&CK for ICS** and turning raw telemetry into actionable detections
- 👥 Proven track record leading SOC shift teams (9-person team), writing SOPs, and building use cases that hold up under audit
- 🤝 Comfortable bridging IT and OT — working across network, system, database, application, and security teams
- 🐍 Building security automation and AI-assisted hunt tooling with Python to scale detection engineering
